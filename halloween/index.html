"HALLOWEEN" Week 5: 10.30.2022

I had been looking forward to my first NYC halloween all year, and I was not disappointed. (While I did live in new york last year during Halloween, I was sick and didn’t have a costume or leave my bed the entire time, so I don’t think last year counted.

This year, I dressed as Calvin and Hobbes from the eponymous comic, which I loved as a child. Look below to see photos of the comic that inspired my costume.

Look below to see what my costume looks like when other people are watching me vs when they aren't. In the Calvin and Hobbes comic, Hobbes is a tiger who is Calvin’s best (imaginary?) friend. When the two are alone together, hobbes is drawn as a real, living tiger. In scenes where other people, such as Clavin’s parents, are present, Hobbes is drawn as a stuffed tiger toy. My goal was to mimic that, buying a stuffed tiger to represent “toy/imaginary” Hobbes, and using my cat Luka to represent the real Hobbes. I didn’t want to put my actual cat in a costume, so just pretend he looks like a tiger.
