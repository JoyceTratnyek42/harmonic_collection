Images + Words: a Scrapbook

by Joyce Tratnyek




The museum of a harmonic collection assingment is this class's semester-long project. For my theme, I have chosen words and images. For each week, I will record a word (or phrase) I came across that week that stood out to me. Then, I will create something based on that: a melody, poem, animation, sketch, etc, which I will psot here. I will also post photos from my week that I believe relate to the words I chose, or simply capture the vibes and feelings of that week. This is designed to be an experimental, open-ended scrapbook/bulletin board and way for me to capture a time in my life using the most basic of media: pictures and text.
