"REBEL"

Week 1: 10.2.2022




I saw this word on an instagram post by an environmental justice organization. Although I’ve heard this word countless times in my life, something about it just stuck out to me in that moment. I started thinking about what it means to be a rebel.

According to Dictionary.com, a rebel is someone who “refuses allegiance to, resists, or rises in arms against the government or ruler of their country” or “a person who resists any authority, control, or tradition.” I don’t necessarily agree with either of those definitions, the first one seeming too narrow and the second seeming too broad for me. I don’t think rebels have to resist ANY control of tradition, some rebels have very specific causes that they fight for. And I don’t think that rebels need to necessarily fight against their specific country or government, it is possible to rebel against issues that are either quite local and informal, or that cross national borders.
